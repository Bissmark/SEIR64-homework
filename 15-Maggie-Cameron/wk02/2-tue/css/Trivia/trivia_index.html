Margaret Hamilton

Trivia

Hometown Home FAQ
  • In 2016 Hamilton was awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom by Barack Obama for her work on NASA’s Apollo missions

  • As a junior at MIT Hamilton solved an ‘impossible’ prank assignment. The results were quickly adopted by the military to develop the first aircraft defence systems

  • In 2017 Hamilton was immortalised as a NASA Lego figurine

  • The character ‘Margo Maddison’ in the popular series ‘For all Mankind’ is based on Hamilton. The creator was inspired by the photo to the right, of Hamilton standing next to her Apollo software that guided men to the moon.
